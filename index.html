What is Hackatron ?



Hackatron is an attempt made by The Developer's Society, the official coding club of B.I.T. Sindri, to bring out the developer hidden inside a student, by providing a platform to showcase the skills and stand out of the crowd. We belive that given proper resources, anything is possible. The raison d'Ãªtre of Hackatron is to introduce students to the open source community and its benefits. In Hackatron, participants are encouraged to come up with novel ideas and collaborate with each other to design unique solutions to some real world problems. We believe that people with passion can change the world for the better and that those people who are crazy enough to think that they can change the world, are the ones that actually do. So, it is a call for all the geeks out there, come, be a part of this event and make a dent in this universe. All you have to do is to DESING,DEBUG and DEPLOY.

Team can consist of minimum 2 members and a maximum of 4 members.
The fooding and lodging is absolutely free to the teams reaching the final on-campus round being held at BIT Sindri, Dhanbad. You will enjoy the snacking sessions between your journey of hacking.
